CENTER POSITION

The center and power forward positions are two of the five standard positions in basketball. The game can be significantly affected by these positions, which are primarily in charge of playing close to the basket. The center typically stands the tallest on the team and is positioned in the low post, close to the basket. Their main duties include grabbing rebounds, guarding the basket, and setting screens to open up space for their teammates. In addition to being good at blocking shots on defense, centers are frequently skilled at scoring close to the basket.

They can form a powerful frontcourt that can exert control over the game on both ends of the court. They are in charge of scoring, defending, and grabbing rebounds close to the basket.
